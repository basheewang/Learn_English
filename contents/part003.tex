\subsection{\(-ed\)形容词和\(-ing\)形容词}

以 -ed 结尾的形容词和以 -ing 结尾的形容词用法有所不同，要注意区分。
\begin{enumerate}
  \item 以-ed结尾的形容词通常用于说明人，描述人的主观感受。比如:She felt exhausted after a whole day of intense academic research.
  \item 以-ing结尾的形容词通常用于说明事物，描述事物的性质或特征，表示事物对人的情感能产生相应的影响。如：The historical documentary is so engaging that I watched it twice.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{形容词表达感受和观点的句式}

常用的两个句式：
\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{表达感受}：主语+be+形容词+that. 如：She's delighted that she has been admitted to her dream university.
  \item \textbf{表达可能性和观点}：It+be+形容词+that. 如：It's obvious that excessive use of cellphone has a negative impact on our eyesight.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{比较级的强调用法}


若想对比较级进行强调，可采用以下方式:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item 日常交流中，常使用even、far、a great deal、a little、a lot、much 等词来修饰比较级,以增强语气，如much greater和much simpler。再比如:The problem is much more complex than we expected.
  \item 在正式英语语境下，常见的修饰比较级的词有 slightly、considerably、significantly等,实现强调效果，如:The new policy has made the environment significantly better.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{常用比较级的句式}

“比较级句式”在考试中应用广泛,尤其在写作中,分析数据、对比观点或强调差异都常用到。
\begin{enumerate}
  \item “the+比较级，the+比较级”表示“越……，越……”，比如:The sooner you start, the earlier you will finish.
  \item “比较级+and+比较级”表示“越来越…”，比如more and more important，再比如:The city is getting more and more prosperous.
  \item “比较级+ than + any other+可数名词单数”表示“……比同一范围的任何一个人/物都要……”，比如:Mount Qomolangma is higher than any other mountain in the world.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{“too \(\ldots\) to” 结构 }

\begin{enumerate}
  \item 结构“too+形容词/副词+to do”表示“太……而不能……”。注意该结构表达的是否定含义。比如:The concept is too abstract to be easily understood by beginners.
  \item 与该结构意思容易混淆的短语还有:
        \begin{enumerate}
          \item “so \(\ldots\) that”表示“如此……以至于……”。比如:The environmental problems are so urgent that we must take immediate action.
          \item “not \(\ldots\) enough to”表示“不够……以至于不能……”。比如:His language skills are not good enough to study abroad without extra training.
        \end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{数词倍数表达}

在考试中，描述数据、趋势或进行比较时，常会涉及“倍数”。常用的“倍数”表达方式有以下几种:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item 倍数+形容词比较级+than+比较对象 (实际倍数是比较级所表示的程度加上1): This room is twice larger than that one. 这个房间比那个房间大两倍。 (这个房间是那个房间的三倍大)。
  \item 倍数 + as \(\ldots\) as \(\ldots\)： This box is four times as heavy as that one.
  \item 倍数+the size/number/amount/weight/length of \(\ldots\)：This company produces twice the amount of waste as its competitor.
  \item 倍数+what 从句：The population of this city is three times what it was ten years ago.
\end{enumerate}
\subsection{数词 \(double\)}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item double可作形容词，表示“两倍的；双份的”，后面可接名词、数词、代词，比如:a double espresso
  \item double也可作限定词，表示“两倍的，双倍的”，后面可接定冠词the 或物主代词等，有时也可接what引导的从句，比如:The production is now double what it was ten
        years ago.
  \item double还可作动词，表示“是……的两倍;使加倍”，比如:The output has been doubled in the past five years.
  \item 以下是关于“倍数”的一些常用表达。
        \begin{enumerate}
          \item 两倍:double,twice
          \item 三倍:three times,triple
          \item 四倍:four times, fourfold
          \item 五倍:five times
        \end{enumerate}
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{\(few\)和\(little\)}

few 和 little、a few 和alittle 看起来相似，也都用于描述数量，但含义却有很大不同。
\begin{enumerate}
  \item few和little 具有否定含义，表示数量很少，几乎没有，往往给人一种比较消极的感觉。比如:He has few chances to win the game.
  \item a few和a little具有肯定含义,表示虽然数量不多,但还是有一些,语气相对积极。比如:We still have a few minutes left before the train leaves.
\end{enumerate}
